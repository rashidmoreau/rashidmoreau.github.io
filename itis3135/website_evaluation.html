Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's official website is a prime example of a well-executed design.

  • Contrast: Excellent use of high contrast between text and background to enhance readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent branding and design elements across all pages, reinforcing familiarity and professionalism.
  • Alignment: Elements are perfectly aligned, contributing to a clean and organized appearance that simplifies navigation.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped logically, ensuring users can easily find relevant information.

Arngren.net

Arngren.net serves as an example of poor web design.

  • Contrast: Subpar contrast makes text difficult to read against various backgrounds.
  • Repetition: Little to no repetition in design elements results in a chaotic and confusing aesthetic.
  • Alignment: Inconsistent alignment creates visual disorder that detracts from usability.
  • Proximity: Related items are scattered across the page, making navigation difficult.
