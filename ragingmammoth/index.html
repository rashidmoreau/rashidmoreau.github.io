Welcome to Raging Mammoth

Soup on a stick? You heard that right! At Raging Mammoth, we've revolutionized the way you enjoy your favorite comfort food. Our solidified, flavorful broth cubes are skewered and dipped into hot water, melting into a delicious, on-the-go soup experience.

Choose from a variety of flavors, from classic chicken noodle to spicy Thai curry. Perfect for busy days, outdoor adventures, and anyone who loves a warm meal without the mess.
