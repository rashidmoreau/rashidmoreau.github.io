About Raging Mammoth

At Raging Mammoth, we believe in revolutionizing the way people enjoy soup. Born from a passion for convenient, delicious, and mess-free meals, our unique soup-on-a-stick concept has taken comfort food to a whole new level.

Our journey began with a simple idea: how can we make soup more accessible without sacrificing taste? Through innovative solidified broth cubes that dissolve instantly in hot water, we’ve created a fun, portable, and satisfying way to enjoy your favorite flavors anywhere.

Join us in redefining soup culture—one stick at a time!
